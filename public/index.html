Quality Construction

Our pillow covers are characterized by...

  • Perfect pattern match. Nothing gives a professional, finished look to a PoppyPillows pillow cover like a perfect pattern match. We make sure our seams appear in as unbroken a manner as possible, aiming for a balanced, harmonious effect.
  • Craftmanship. Each of our covers is handcrafted, uses backstitching in seams and other important areas so that they are durable. All our covers are made in the USA.

Buying & Care Tips

How to Save $$$ and Space on Decorative Pillows

Buy a pair of pillow inserts and change out your pillow covers instead of entire pillows. When you want to change your decor, simply change the pillow cover(s). Pillow covers take up far less room to store. Plus, they can be washed. Covers save you space and money!

Quality pillow inserts are easy to find and inexpensive. We recommend 20"x20" inserts for our covers.

How to Properly Size, Iron & Stuff Pillow Covers

You might damage your new pillow covers through improper handling while stuffing them with pillow inserts. No matter how well sewn your pillow covers are, too much pressure at the seams can cause them to pull apart. You can prevent damage to your pillow covers by following these tips:

  • Get the proper size of insert. Our standard throw pillow covers are 18"x18". For a firm, plump pillow, use a 17"x17" polyfill insert or a 20"x20" feather/down fill insert.
  • Remove wrinkles with medium heat. Iron/steam out wrinkles by using a medium heat setting.
  • Gently stuff your cover. Gently work your insert into the cover so as to avoid sudden expansion against the seams.
