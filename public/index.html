Quality Construction

Our pillow covers are characterized by...

  • Perfect pattern match. Nothing gives a professional, finished look to a PoppyPillows pillow cover like a perfect pattern match. We make sure our seams appear in as unbroken a manner as possible, aiming for a balanced, harmonious effect.
  • Craftmanship. Each of our covers is handcrafted and uses backstitching in seams and other important areas so that they are durable. All our covers are made in the USA.
  • Limited quantities. No pillow cover design is ever produced in open than a quantity of 50, offering you continual exclusivity and fresh designs.

Buying & Care Tips

How to Save $$$ and Space on Decorative Pillows

Buy a pair of pillow inserts and change out your pillow covers instead of buying entire pillows when you want to change your decor.

Pillow covers take up far less room to store. Plus, they can be washed.

Covers save you space and money!

How to Get the Right Size of Pillow Insert

For a firm, plump pillow, the general rule is to use a pillow insert that is 1"-2" larger than the size of the cover.

All of our pillow covers are 18"x18".

Quality pillow inserts are easy to find and inexpensive. We recommend...

  • 17"x17" polyfill insert, or
  • 20"x20" feather/down insert

How to Properly Stuff & Care for Pillow Covers

You might damage your new pillow covers through improper handling while stuffing them with pillow inserts. No matter how well sewn your pillow covers are, too much pressure at the seams can cause them to pull apart. You can prevent damage to your pillow covers by following these tips:

  • Gently stuff your cover. Gently work your insert into the cover to avoid unnecessary pressure on the seams.
  • Washing and drying. Wash your covers in cool water to preserve color, and let them air dry. If you dry them with a dryer, use a low heat setting.
  • Ironing. Iron/steam out wrinkles by using a medium heat setting.
