Buying & Care Tips

How to Get the Right Size of Pillow Insert

For a firm, plump pillow, the general rule is to use a pillow insert that is 1"-2" larger than the size of the cover.

All of our pillow covers are 18"x18".

Quality pillow inserts are easy to find and inexpensive. We recommend...

  • 17"x17" polyfill insert, or
  • 20"x20" feather/down insert

How to Save $$$ and Space on Decorative Pillows

Buy a pair of pillow inserts and change out your pillow covers instead of buying entire pillows when you want to change your decor.

Pillow covers take up far less room to store. Plus, they can be washed.

Covers save you space and money!

How to Properly Stuff & Care for Pillow Covers

You might damage your new pillow covers through improper handling while stuffing them with pillow inserts. No matter how well sewn your pillow covers are, too much pressure at the seams can cause them to pull apart. You can prevent damage to your pillow covers by following these tips:

  • Gently stuff your cover. Gently work your insert into the cover to avoid unnecessary pressure on the seams.
  • Washing and drying. Wash your covers in cool water to preserve color, and let them air dry. If you dry them with a dryer, use a low heat setting.
  • Ironing. Iron/steam out wrinkles by using a medium heat setting.

The Most Affordable Way to Decorate with Throw Pillows

So, the season has changed, and you're going to change the pillow decor in your living room or on your bed. Are you thinking, "Where am I going to put all those pillows?!"

Have you already started a collection in your closet? Are you losing storage space?!

Or perhaps you're just starting out. You realize that pillows are not cheap. You need your place to be stylish and cozy, but you're on a budget — space-wise and money-wise. What can you do?

Top 3 Reasons to Use Pillow Covers for Decor

Purchasing quality pillows inserts, such as down/feather filled ones, and then using quality pillow covers (like ours) will meet your pillow decor needs.

  • Save $$$. Don't buy sets of pillows for decor. Simply buy pillow inserts once, and change only the pillow covers. Covers are less expensive than entire pillows.
  • Save storage space. Pillow covers are foldable and flat. You can store multiple sets in a drawer, whereas you need lots of closet space to store entire pillows.
  • Keep them clean. Did someone spill food on your cover? Did a pet make a mess? Pillow covers are removable and washable. Entire pillows are not!

Whatever your situation, decorating with pillows is a great way to add color and comfort to your space... and you don't have to break your budget or have loads of closet space.
